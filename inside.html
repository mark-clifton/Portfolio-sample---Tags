Daycare We Put Pet-Parents At Ease

What furniture is he going to chew up today? Is she going to lick the light socket again? These are questions you shouldn’t have to ask or answer, especially when you are trying to focus on a busy day at work or getting errands done before heading home.

TAGS! Pet Care Club is in the business of stress reduction—yours and your pet’s. We understand how pet lovers like you worry about your dog or cat home alone. And we know all about the mischief pets can bring if they have separation anxiety or just plain miss their human mom or dad.

The Facilities

TAGS! Pet Care Club is a cage-free facility. You may need a cubicle at work, but your dog doesn’t need one at play. That’s why we have 3, 20-foot by 20-foot play areas decorated with white-picket fencing, a raised garden, and paw-friendly “turf” (similar to tennis court material). Web cams are placed in each play area, so you can keep virtual track of your pet at any time of day.

When your dog is not at play, he or she is taken to a personal suite for napping, eating and drinking, or just chilling. Suites are 4-foot by 7-foot and 6-foot by 7-foot for larger dogs. Each unit comes with a comfy bed and bowls for food and water. We believe in individualized care, so if your dog has a particular feeding schedule, we will gladly stick to it.

For cats, we offer a spacious 10-foot by 12-foot play room with plenty of ceiling height and fixtures to climb.

The Other Pets

At TAGS! Pet Care Club, your pet will be matched with dogs based on 3 factors:

  • Size
  • Play style
  • Energy style

Just because a dog is larger, doesn’t mean he or she is looking for raucous play. Just because a dog is older doesn’t mean he or she is calmer than some pups. Size, age, and breed (typical play style) matter, but so does energy style. Our staff will assess your dog at intake and determine which play area is best suited for him or her.

The Care Providers

Every pet caregiver at TAGS! will be certified in Pet CPR and First Aid. But that’s only the most basic qualification we’ll be looking for. We want pet lovers who understand the difference between doing the job and taking the extra step for our customers—you and your pet.

We believe in individualized care and our goal is to have a stress-free experience for you and your pet. We are committed to only hiring staff members whose love for animals make them not just supervise and feed your beloved pet, but connect with them on an emotional level, too.

We know your pets will be happy at TAGS! Pet Care Club, but we also know we cannot replace the love you give them at home. So while you’re away, we’ll do our best to make sure your dog or cat feels the human bond they crave. It starts with loving caregivers.

The Program

TAGS! Pet Care Club provides 2 supervisors for each play area. That’s a 12:1 supervisor-to-pet ratio, significantly lower than industry norms. Individualized suites will be regularly monitored to make sure your pet is fed, clean, safe, and content. Daycare hours are 6 a.m. to 7 p.m.

Rates are:

  • $35 for a full day
  • $25 for a half day

You can save significantly by purchasing 10-day passes. Full-day 10-day passes are $300 and half-day 10-day passes are $200.

Ask about our PAWtners plans where a second dog is just $21 for a full day and $15 for a half day.

We will gladly take your pet to any veterinary appointment he or she may have during that time. If your animal needs immediate veterinary care, we partner with South Loop Animal Hospital just 5 minutes away.
